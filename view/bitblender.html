Hi Friends
So the bitcoin global renderfarm has started to take shape. This is just the beginning, this is a closed secret beta. Just till everything is working and we test for bugs. Okay.. the basic steps would be something like this:

STEP 1 Upload your zipped blender scene folder.
Our system then calculates a recommended amount of bitcoin to get your job rendered.
STEP 2 (Optional) Increase your offered job reward to incentivize the render nodes to work on your queued job before others, or decrease the reward to render for cheaper, but might take longer if the reward is too low!
STEP 3 Send the bitcoin to be held securely while your job is rendered. Workers will start automatically as bitcoin is deposited to your account. BitBlender dispenses the bitcoin reward to workers on a per frame basis. You can see the frames as they come in and have a chance to cancel the remaining frames incase you see a problem in the rendered result!
